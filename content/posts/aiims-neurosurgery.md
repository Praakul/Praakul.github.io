### The Internship
At the Department of Neurosurgery, AIIMS Delhi, I engineered an agentic AI framework to evaluate cylindrical suturing samples using multi-modal mechanisms.

### Key Technical Contributions
- **Interpretability:** Integrated Grad-CAM visualizations to provide "Explainable AI" for surgeons.
- **Sensor Fusion:** Implemented eye-tracking integration for endoscopy fusion in surgical navigation.
- **Automation:** Built a standalone application for evaluating craniotomy training samples with automated report generation.
